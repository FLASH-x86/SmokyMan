What Smoking Does to Your Body

Lungs: Filled with tar, reducing oxygen flow.

Heart: Increased risk of heart disease & stroke.

Brain: Higher chances of addiction & reduced cognition.

Skin: Early aging, dullness, and damage.

How Can I Quit Smoking?

1) Make a Quit Plan: Having a plan helps you stay focused and motivated.

2) Stay Busy: Keeps your mind off cravings and distractions.

3) Avoid Triggers: Stay away from things that make you want to smoke.

4) Stay Positive: One day at a time. It adds up.

5) Ask for Help: Tell your friends and family. Let them support you.
